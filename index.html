About Us!

In the last decade Alan has expanded the business working for many local and international businesses. From opening up its Headquarters in Newry, Northern Ireland, to creating impressive links with massive global companies.

Hot Beans Web was founded in 2006 by Alan Tichmarsh. After having years of experience  working with other website developing businesses, he decided to set up Hot Beans Web after he felt that other website developer companies, he was working with weren't intuitive enough. He swore an oath, every website made by Hot Beans Web will be created with the highest of standard!
