Job Specifications
   
   
 
Qualifications

To be able to work as a Web Developer for Hot Beans Web you will need some knowledge in:

  • GCSE in Art (or BTEC equivalent).
  • Level 2 award in Creating a Website.
  • Level 2 Certificate in Interactive Media.
  • Level 2/3 Diploma in Creative iMedia.
  • Level 3 Diploma in Information Technology (Specialist).
  • Level 3 Diploma in IT User Skills.
  
   
 
What we expect from you
   
    Whilst working with Hot Beans Web, we expect that your major responsibility will be to construct dependable and high-performing web-based application and services. You are expected to produce products that fulfil the needs of clients. You’ll meet with clients to provide status updates. We are looking for candidates with high ambition, good communication skills, time management skills, a continuously learning attitude and ideally testing skills.
